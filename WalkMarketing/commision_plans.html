What We Offer

CPA

Our Cost Per Acquisition assures that you get a fixed payment for every customer you refer to WalkMarketing. The CPA commission structure insures a consistent rate for every new trader

Dynamic CPA

In case of bringing a large customer, this program will allow you to take a percentage of the trader’s First Time Deposit (FTD) – bigger the deposit more you receive

RevShare

You can continue to receive your revenue share and see profits from your customer’s trading volume, as long as they keep trading – a fixed percentage belongs to you

Master Affiliate

If you want to refer customers or affiliates, you can do either or both – and get paid for your customers’ traffic and trading, as well as a fixed percentage of your sub-affiliates’ performance – as suitable for your needs
